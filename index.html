RGB Party!

Click the canvas to get started. Hit 'r', 'g', and 'b' on your keyboard to create red, green, and blue food, respectively, where your mouse is, attracting particles of the same color!

Additional Controls:
Hold 'r', 'g', or 'b' and drag your mouse to move the food in real time.
Hit 'shift' + 'r', 'g', or 'b' to remove the food of the corresponding color.
Hit 'w' to remove all food and hit 'q' to randomize the positions of all particles.
